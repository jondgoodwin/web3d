Avatar Type

An Avatar is a purposeful, active, interactive object, animated by a user or AI. Each Avatar specifies everything about what they look like, are wearing, carrying, can do, etc. It includes their state, position, class, and "inventory" (spells, bags, armor, shopping cart, ...), timers, restrictions, etc.

Methods and Properties

Methods do things or change things (cast spells, change state, take or lose possessions, etc.

eye
Position and orientation of the actor's eye, which you can have a camera follow.
