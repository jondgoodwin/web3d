Pegasus3D Browser for exploring 3-D worlds

Free & Open Source 3-D Browser

Pegasus3D leaps over the world-wide web's flat pages, flying you to virtual worlds(*), rich with things to see and do.

Click-travel through three-dimensional worlds designed for you to explore, learn, play, shop, and socialize with others.

Or build your own 3-D world out of modular parts, accessible to all Internet visitors, interconnected to other worlds, and unrestricted by licensing fees and proprietary lock-ins.

The real world is not flat. Why should online adventures be?
